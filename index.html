Welcome to Wisp & Willows


We are a small company based in Aberdeen, creating handcrafted lamps and beautiful homeware.


All my lamps are crafted through a combination of design and intuition. I always seek to preserve the original natural shape of the wood I use and enhance its beauty using natural oils and waxes.
